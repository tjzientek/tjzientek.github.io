T.J. Zientek

T.J. Zientek is the Director, Information Technology at Morgan Samuels and partners with the Executive Team to recommend, design and implement innovative software solutions tailored to the retained executive search industry. He is responsible for capturing and analyzing key data in the areas of finance, quality, and research, and ensures that the firm remains on the cutting edge of technology to achieve key business objectives and allow remote employees to work together seamlessly.

Previously, T.J. worked for over 20 years in the mortgage industry, as a major part of the regulatory and compliance team. He has served as an IT Manager and Software Developer for Absolute Disclosure, 1st Capital Home Mortgage, and eHomeCredit.
